Subject: Proposal for Partnership to Support One Talented Japanese High School Student

Dear 大学名,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is Yoshinao Ebisawa, and I am the coordinator of the WA Program, an initiative dedicated to fostering global opportunities for outstanding Japanese high school students through academic scholarships and international partnerships.

Last year, we successfully partnered with 26 universities, including Beloit College, Hope College, Hofstra University, and Whittier College, enabling talented students from Japan to study abroad with substantial tuition support. These scholarships, generously provided by our partner universities, covered up to 80% of tuition costs, making education abroad accessible to our brightest students.

Building on this success, we are seeking to expand our collaborations with universities offering scholarships that cover 100% of tuition costs from 2025-2026 admission for one excellent Japanese student. We believe that such support will open doors for even more exceptional students to contribute their skills and perspectives to your campus community.

For living expenses, including accommodation fees, students will be responsible for their own costs, or we will work to secure sponsorships from Japanese companies.

We are confident that this partnership can be mutually beneficial: your university will gain highly motivated and talented students with diverse backgrounds, while these students will have the opportunity to grow academically and personally in your esteemed institution.

We would be delighted to discuss this opportunity further at your convenience. Please let us know if you are open to exploring this collaboration.

Thank you for considering this proposal. We look forward to the possibility of working together to support the next generation of global leaders.
