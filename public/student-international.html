What We Offer

Two pathways to
scholarship funding

University Scholarships

Aid issued directly by your institution

Many Japanese universities offer merit-based and need-based scholarships specifically for international students. These awards are issued under the university's name and can often be combined with external grants. Search by university, field of study, or award amount.

Browse University Aid →
Japanese Foundation Scholarships

Grants from Japan's leading foundations

Japan has a rich ecosystem of private foundations — JASSO, Rotary, Okazaki Kaheita, and dozens of others — that award scholarships to international students studying in the country. We aggregate these opportunities and surface the ones relevant to you.

How to Use This Resource

Three steps to
finding your scholarship

01 — Search

Filter by your situation

Use the scholarship search to filter by type, university, field of study, nationality eligibility, and language requirements. No account required.

02 — Review

Check eligibility & deadlines

Each listing includes award amount, application deadline, eligibility criteria, and a direct link to the official application page.

03 — Apply

Apply directly

We link you directly to the official scholarship provider. All applications are submitted through the foundation or university — Will Abroad is a discovery tool, not an intermediary.
