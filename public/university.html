Program Period

Students enrolled in September 2025 and will complete their four-year undergraduate degree in June 2029.

Scholarship Range

50% tuition reduction
up to full coverage

Scholarships range from a 50% tuition waiver to full coverage of tuition, dormitory, and dining — negotiated directly with each partner institution on behalf of selected students.

Selection Process

Rigorous two-stage selection

Applicants are evaluated through a document review (transcripts, English proficiency scores, and essays) followed by an online interview. Only candidates who best match each university's culture and expectations are recommended.

Eligibility is intentionally broad: any Japanese-fluent student without a bachelor's degree may apply, regardless of age, nationality, or school type — including gap-year students, homeschoolers, and community college enrollees.

Why Will Abroad

What we offer
your institution

01

Pre-Screened, Enrollment-Ready Students

Every candidate we refer has cleared a rigorous two-stage selection — document review plus online interview. Your admissions team receives a shortlist of qualified, motivated applicants, not an open pool.

02

Scholarship Issued by Your Institution

Will Abroad is not a foundation. We negotiate a scholarship framework directly with you, and the award is issued under your institution's name — giving you full control over terms and allowing students to stack external awards.

03

An Expanding Global Network

We launched with U.S. universities in 2024 and are actively building partnerships beyond North America. Joining now positions your institution at the front of a growing pipeline of Japanese undergraduate talent.
