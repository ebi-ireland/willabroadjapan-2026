About Will Abroad

Making education
financially possible

Will Abroad is a Japanese study-abroad organization originally founded to help Japanese students access international universities through negotiated scholarships. Today we also serve international students in Japan — helping them discover funding that makes their studies here sustainable.

Our Mission

Education should not be
limited by financial means

What We Believe

Talent is universal. Opportunity is not.

We believe that financial constraints should not determine who gets access to quality education. Will Abroad exists to close the gap between academic ambition and financial reality — whether that means a Japanese student studying abroad, or an international student making the most of their time in Japan.

For International Students

A free, transparent scholarship resource

We aggregate university-specific financial aid and Japanese foundation scholarships into a single, searchable database. There are no fees to use this resource, no commissions, and no intermediary steps — we connect you directly to the official scholarship source.

Who We Serve

International students
at every stage

Undergraduate Students

Bachelor's degree programs

Whether you're in your first year or approaching graduation, there are scholarship opportunities available at each stage of your undergraduate journey in Japan.

Graduate Students

Master's & Doctoral programs

Graduate-level scholarships in Japan often carry higher award amounts and longer duration. Japanese foundations are particularly active in supporting advanced research.

Any Nationality

Open to all international students

While some scholarships are region-specific, many Japanese foundation and university awards are open to students of all nationalities currently enrolled in a Japanese institution.

Our History

Founded in Japan.
Built for global students.

2023 — Founded

Will Abroad was established in Japan to help Japanese students access international universities through negotiated scholarship frameworks. In 2024, our inaugural cohort saw 26 students enroll across 26 U.S. partner universities.

2025 — Expanded

Recognizing the parallel challenge faced by international students in Japan — navigating a fragmented landscape of scholarship opportunities — we launched this resource to serve the inbound student community as well.
